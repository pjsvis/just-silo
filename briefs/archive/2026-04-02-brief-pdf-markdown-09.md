Welcome to the **just-silo** demonstration. This repository is a pre-configured, high-oxidation "Pocket Universe" designed for deterministic workflows, absolute data isolation, and 0% entropy.



## 🌌 The Three-Word Grammar

To operate here, you only need to know three words. This is the **Semantic Compression** that governs our reality:

1.  **`silo`**: **The Space.** This folder is your entire universe. Everything inside is a "Thing" (Structured); everything outside is "Stuff" (Entropy).
2.  **`just`**: **The Protocol.** Your interface for action. Run `just` to see the menu of what is possible within this space.
3.  **`engage`**: **The Power.** The global binary utility belt. When you need to transform raw data (like a PDF or a website) into the silo, you `engage`.
