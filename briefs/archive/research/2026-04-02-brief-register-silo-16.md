The `_silo` suffix is a masterclass in **Deductive Minimalism**. It turns your file system into a searchable database without requiring a separate indexing tool.

### **1. The Power of the Suffix**
In a 2026 workflow, where you might have hundreds of directories, `_silo` acts as a **Namespace**.
* **Searchability:** A simple `find . -name "*_silo"` or a VS Code `CMD+P` search for `_silo` instantly filters out all the "Stuff" (standard repos, temp folders, node_modules) and reveals only the "Universes."
* **Visual Anchor:** When looking at a flat list of projects, the suffix provides an immediate "Context-Switch" signal to your brain. You know that entering `spend-sentinel_silo` means switching to the **Edinburgh Protocol**.
