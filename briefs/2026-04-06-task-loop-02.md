### **1. Executive Summary**
The objective is to move beyond "Chat-based" AI interactions by implementing a **Task-Loop** architecture within the Silo Framework. This pattern treats a folder not just as a storage unit, but as a **Stateful CPU**. By reducing all operations to **Cron** (periodic) and **File** (reactive) triggers, we eliminate orchestration complexity and ensure that all AI "Mentation" is restartable, idempotent, and auditable.

---

### **2. The Primary Primitives**

#### **A. The Task-as-Substrate (`td-todo-task`)**
The `td-todo-task` is the **Loom of Memory**. It is a persistent JSON object that serves as the "Instruction Pointer" for the agent.
* **Storage:** Every task has a unique ID and a corresponding workspace in `/process/<task-id>/`.
* **State:** Agents attach **Notes** (Mentation Artefacts) and **Links** (Data Artefacts) directly to the task.
* **Handoff:** If a task stalls, the state is preserved. A **Try-Harder Agent** can resume the process by reading the task's history.

#### **B. The Two Trigger Types**
To reduce domain complexity, all work is initiated by the OS primitives:
* **File-Triggered (The Reflex):** Data enters `inbox/` or a sub-agent writes a `.done` flag. This triggers an immediate **Task-Agent**.
* **Cron-Triggered (The Heartbeat):** A periodic scan (e.g., every 5 mins) identifies "Zombie" tasks, evaluates cost envelopes, or initiates the **Gamma-Loop** for playbook updates.

### **4. Classification of Tasks**
Every "Verb" in a task-specific `justfile` must be classified to optimize the **Performance Envelope**:
* **Generative (Heuristic):** Creating "Stuff" (Code/Writing). High cost, high reasoning required.
* **Evaluative (Deterministic):** Validating "Things" (Testing/Linting). Low cost, usually handled by **Bun Binaries**.

---

### **5. Definition of Done (DoD) & Entropy Exit**
The Process-Loop exits only when:
1.  **Evaluative Pass:** The task-specific `justfile` returns `exit 0` on all validation verbs.
2.  **Semantic Compression:** High-entropy "Scratchpad" notes are flensed into a final, low-entropy summary.
3.  **Escalate-to-Sovereign:** If the task exceeds its cost or time envelope, the agent **Refrains** from further entropy (token burn) and logs the item for human intervention.
