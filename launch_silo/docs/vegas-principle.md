# The Vegas Principle

**What happens in silo, stays in silo.**

**Date: 2026-03-31**

---

## The Rule

> **WHAT HAPPENS IN SILO, STAYS IN SILO.**
>
> Las Vegas said it first. We say it too.

---

## What This Means

| Category | In Silo | Out of Silo |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Private work** | Private | Not shared |
| **Draft strategy** | Private | Not shared |
| **Internal debates** | Private | Not shared |
| **Launch plans** | Private | Not shared |
| **Lessons learned** | Maybe | Not yet |
| **Published outputs** | — | Public |
| **Open source work** | — | Public |

---

## The Distinction

### General (just-silo, public)

Anyone can see. Anyone can adopt.

- The Silo concept
- The justfile pattern
- The vocabulary
- The bounded context principle
- CAW-Canny (as a coined term, even if silo-specific)
- The Zones of Thought (as Vinge reference)

### Silo-Specific (silo_launch, private)

Only this silo. Only this team.

- Vinge touchstone (our implementation of it)
- Zone transition protocol (our implementation)
- Blast radius enforcement (our implementation)
- Launch strategy
- Confidential plans
- Internal debates
- Draft outputs
- Unpublished lessons

---

## The Vegas Checklist

**Before sharing outside silo:**

Is this general or silo-specific?

- General: Safe to share
- Silo-Specific: Keep in silo
- Not Sure: Ask first

---

## The Lesson

> **Silo-specific is silo-specific.**

The Zones of Thought is Vinge's idea. CAW-Canny is our coinage. But *how we implement* the Zones of Thought, *how we enforce* blast radius, *what we do* in our silo — that's silo-specific.

### just-silo (public)

- General concepts
- Pattern documentation
- Open source

### silo_launch (private)

- Vinge touchstone (our implementation)
- Zone transition (our protocol)
- Blast radius (our enforcement)
- Launch strategy
- Confidential work

---

## The Boundary

**INSIDE (silo-specific, private)**

- Confidential strategy
- Draft plans
- Internal debates
- Implementation details
- Lessons we're still learning

**What flows out:**

- Published outputs
- Open source contributions
- General lessons (when ready)

**OUTSIDE (general, public)**

- The Silo pattern
- The vocabulary
- Bounded context principle
- The Zones of Thought reference
