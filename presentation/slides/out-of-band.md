# Out of Band

## Scenario: "What was that slide?"

**Audience:** "You mentioned something about cost earlier — which slide was that?"

**Presenter:** "Good catch — PXY, show us the agenda."

*PXY shows agenda*

**Presenter:** "Right — Cost Projection was slide 7. PXY, show it."

*PXY shows slide 7*

---

## PXY Commands

- `slides` — List all available slides
- `agenda` — Show the agenda
- `slide 7` — Jump to specific slide
- `back` — Return to previous position

---

## The Point

PXY has full slide inventory.
Audience can ask about anything.
Presenter doesn't need to remember slide numbers.
