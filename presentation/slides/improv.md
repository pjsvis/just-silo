# Improv Mode

## The Demo Within the Demo

Audience asks a question.
↓
PXY generates real-time content.
↓
Content appears instantly.
↓
Presenter interprets.
↓
Back to slides.

---

## Example

**Audience:** "What's the cost for 50k files?"

**Presenter:** "PXY, cost estimate for 50k files."

**PXY:** `./push "# Cost: 50k Files\n\n..."`

*[Content appears on screen]*

**Presenter:** "As you can see..."

---

## Why This Works

- Proves PXY is live, not scripted
- Shows real-time reasoning
- Demonstrates voice control
- Impresses the audience

*"The presentation IS the demo."*
